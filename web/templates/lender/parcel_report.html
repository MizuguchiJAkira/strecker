Methodology
Density estimated via the Random Encounter Model (Rowcliffe et al. 2008) from {{ stats.total_events }} independent detections across {{ stats.n_cameras }} cameras over {{ stats.season_days }} survey days. Confidence intervals via 1,000-iteration nonparametric bootstrap over cameras with truncated-normal perturbation of published daily-travel-distance values. Tier assignment per Mayer & Brisbin 2009 bins: <2 Low · 2–5 Moderate · 5–10 Elevated · ≥10 Severe (animals/km²). Cameras at non-random placements (feeder, trail, water) violate REM's movement-independence assumption; inverse propensity weighting corrects for residual bias but does not eliminate it.
