How it works

1

Set up your property

Draw your property boundary on a satellite map, then drop pins for each camera station. Tag placement context — feeder, trail, water source, food plot — so the pipeline can correct for observation bias in your data.

See camera setup →
2

Upload your photos

Drag in your SD card folder or a ZIP. The detection pipeline runs MegaDetector + SpeciesNet to classify every frame, group burst sequences, and extract independent events — all processed locally in your browser.

Try uploading →
3

Get your dashboard

Species inventories with event counts, 24-hour activity heatmaps, buck-to-doe ratios, camera leaderboards, network coverage scores, a searchable photo gallery, and individual deer re-identification tracking.

View demo dashboard →

What you get

Species Inventory

Every species on your property, ranked by independent events with confidence scores and activity patterns.

Photo Gallery

Browse every detection with species and camera filters, search by date, and page through thousands of photos.

Deer Re-ID

Track individual deer across cameras over time. Upload reference photos, tag bucks, and build a herd profile.

Camera Network Scoring

Density, diversity, distribution, and temporal coverage scored 0–100 with a letter grade and placement recommendations.

Activity Patterns

24-hour activity heatmaps per species. See who's nocturnal, crepuscular, or diurnal on your land.

Season-over-Season Trends

Compare species activity across seasons. Track population changes, new arrivals, and disappearances year over year.
